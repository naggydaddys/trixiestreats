info

Hours of operation vary from day to day.

The truck is parked for winter from December 1st-mid March. Orders can still placed for pick up or delivery during this time.

Trixie's Treats Truck is mobile and can be found throughout the Lehigh Valley. To try some of our delicious sweet treats, you can find our locations under "Find the Truck."

We have about 30 flavors but usually carry 6 on the truck when we are out for an event. Please note, we can only offer you the flavors we have on the menu that day.

history

I come from a long line of women who love to bake! I learned from watching my mom in the kitchen as I was growing up. She in turn, learned from her mom, my grandmother, who came to this country in 1950 from Austria.

The love for the kitchen and producing delicious food was passed down through generations of women in my family. My grandmother learned watching her mom, and my mom learned watching hers, and I, of course, did the same.

I hope you enjoy eating my creations as much as I enjoy making them for you!

Have a sweet day! -Trixie

Things to keep in mind

Events with the truck incur a fee (which varies according to mileage and duration) that includes us serving your guests the cupcake flavors you have chosen.

The cupcakes cost $30/dozen and there is a 3 dozen minimum in addition to the truck fee. We can recommend how many cupcakes to order for the number of guests you are having.

If at all possible, we try to avoid parking the truck on grass areas. The truck is equipped with a full kitchen full of appliances and is very heavy. We would not want to get stuck or be responsible for destroying your yard, so please understand that grass areas are usually not an option.

Please note that a delivery charge of $5 will be added to your total if we deliver an order to you.
